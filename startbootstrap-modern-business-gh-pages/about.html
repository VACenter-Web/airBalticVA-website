Our founding

airBaltic Virtual was founded in August of 2021 by our CEO, David to showcase the airline of Latvia. Since then, we have been planning everyday on how to make this VA a success. On our journey, we were able to get many staff members our way and complete the founding journey together.

Growth & beyond

We hope that everything we have worked on goes well and the growth and journey to the next phase begins. BTVA is honored to be able to improve in any way by anyone's suggestions. Let us reach our goals together with your help!
